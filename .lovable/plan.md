

# Student Study Planner & Progress Tracker

A dark-themed, modern study management app where students can plan, track, and optimize their study habits — with automatic progress updates and a built-in Pomodoro timer.

---

## 1. Authentication
- Sign up / log in with email & password
- Password reset flow
- User profile (display name, avatar)

## 2. Dashboard
- At-a-glance overview: upcoming tasks, today's schedule, study streak, and weekly stats
- Quick-access cards for each subject showing completion percentage
- Motivational progress indicators (streaks, hours studied this week)

## 3. Subject / Course Management
- Create, edit, and delete subjects with name, color, and optional grade goal
- Each subject shows its current grade, task completion rate, and total study hours
- Progress auto-updates as tasks are completed and sessions are logged

## 4. Task & Assignment Tracker
- Add tasks with title, description, due date, priority, and linked subject
- Mark tasks as complete — progress updates automatically
- Filter and sort by due date, priority, subject, or status
- Overdue task alerts

## 5. Study Schedule / Timetable
- Weekly calendar view to plan study blocks
- Drag-friendly time slots assigned to subjects
- View today's schedule on the dashboard

## 6. Pomodoro Study Timer
- Start a focus session linked to a specific subject
- Configurable work/break intervals (default 25/5 min)
- Session auto-logs study time to the subject when completed
- Visual timer with session count

## 7. Progress Tracking & Stats
- Charts showing: study hours per subject, daily/weekly trends, task completion rates
- Overall GPA or grade tracking across subjects
- Streak counter for consecutive study days

## 8. Design & Experience
- Dark theme with vibrant accent colors per subject
- Sleek, modern UI with smooth transitions
- Fully responsive for desktop and mobile
- Sidebar navigation
